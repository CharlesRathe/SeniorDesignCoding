Use sketch FPS_Enroll to enroll new fingers. There is a max of twenty.
It will ask you to place your finger and remove it 3 times. Open serial
monitor to view. It will tell you whether successful.


Use sketch FPS_IDFinger to identify a finger. It will continuosly ask you
to place your finger and if correctly identified will return your finger ID (0-20)


Please add the FPS511C3 library to your arduino libraries. (included)
